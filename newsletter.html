Conversely, traditional retailers that fail to adapt may find themselves at a disadvantage, losing relevance in a rapidly changing market. Moving forward, expect to see more strategic partnerships and acquisitions in the D2C realm, as FMCG companies aim to stay ahead of the curve and align with evolving consumer preferences.
